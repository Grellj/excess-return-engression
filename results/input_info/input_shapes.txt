The flattened X tensor used in Engression is constructed from 4 original tensors: X_past, X_weekday, X_std and X_all. 
The shape of X_past is: 
(2444, 50, 90)
The shape of X_weekday is: 
(2444, 1)
The shape of X_std is: 
(2444, 90)
The shape of X_all is: 
(2444, 8)
Flattening and concatenating these tensors yields X, here called X_engressable with shape: 
(2414, 4599)
It is subsequently split into training, validation and test sets.
The shape of the X-tensor used in training is: 
torch.Size([1931, 4599])
The shape of the Y-tensor used in training is: 
torch.Size([1931, 300])
The shape of the X-tensor used for validation is: 
torch.Size([241, 4599])
The shape of the Y-tensor used for validation is: 
torch.Size([241, 300])
The shape of the X-tensor used for final testing is: 
torch.Size([242, 4599])
The shape of the Y-tensor used for final testing is: 
torch.Size([242, 300])
Note that different batch sizes are being implemented in main.py and thus not displayed here.
